Job Details

We are looking for fresh software engineers to design, develop and maintain applications. You will be responsible for gathering user requirements, defining system functionality and writing code in various languages. Your primary focus will be development of software applications. You will be working alongside other engineers and developers working on different layers of the infrastructure. Therefore, a commitment to collaborative problem solving, sophisticated design, and the creation of quality products is essential.

Job Responsibilities
  • Developing and executing project plans.
  • Manage and execute the software development lifecycle.
  • Write well-designed and testable code.
  • Applying best practices and standard operating procedures.
  • Creating innovative solutions to meet our company’s technical needs.
  • Making documents when required.
  • Make sure 100% bug free product before deployment.
Skills Required
  • Bachelor’s degree in computer sciences (BSCS/SE) or other related fields.
  • Fresh to 1 year of experience is required
  • Excellent programming and analytical skills.
  • Self-driven and desire to excel.
  • Flexible and willing to accept a change in priorities as necessary.

We have an amazing team of qualified individuals working on highly innovative enterprise projects. People who work with us work with the cutting-edge technologies while contributing success to the company as well as to themselves. This is obviously above and beyond the usual stuff (excellent pay, medical benefits and frequent appraisals).
