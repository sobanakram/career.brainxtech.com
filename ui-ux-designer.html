Job Details

BrainX Technologies is looking for a UI/UX Designer to create high end quality designs for our clients. UI/UX Designer responsibilities include gathering user requirements, designing graphic elements and building navigation components. To be successful in this role, you should have experience with design software and wireframe tools. You’ll create both functional and appealing designs that address our clients’ needs and help us grow our customer base.

Job Responsibilities
  • Plans concept by studying information and materials.
  • Illustrate concept by designing rough layout of art and copy regarding arrangement, size, type, style and related aesthetic concepts.
  • Create designs that identify a product or convey a message and present the finalized ideas and concepts to clients.
  • Incorporate changes recommended.
Skills Required
  • Bachelor’s degree in related fields.
  • Fresh to 1 year of experience is required.
  • Knowledge of Illustrator, Photoshop, Adobe XD is preferred.
  • Self-driven and desire to excel.
  • Flexible and willing to accept a change in priorities as necessary.

We have an amazing team of qualified individuals working on highly innovative enterprise projects. People who work with us work with cutting-edge technologies while contributing success to the company as well as to themselves. This is obviously above and beyond the usual stuff (excellent pay, medical benefits and frequent appraisals).
